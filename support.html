Support & NHS Services

If you're struggling or need help, you're not alone. Here are some trusted services.

NHS Mental Health Services

Speak to your GP or self-refer for mental health support in your area.

Website: nhs.uk/mental-health

Phone: 111 (non-emergency), 999 (emergency)

Samaritans

24/7 free helpline if you need someone to talk to confidentially.

Website: samaritans.org

Phone: 116 123 (Free)

Mind UK

Mental health charity offering information and support.

Website: mind.org.uk

Phone: 0300 123 3393

Shout 85258

Free, confidential text support service for anyone in crisis.

Text: 'SHOUT' to 85258

Website: giveusashout.org
